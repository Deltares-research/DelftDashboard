Adding dem or watersheds data: 
1. make sure the names are exactly: 'dem.tif' or 'watersheds.gpkg'
2. copy them to FloodAdaptModelBuilder\\data\\topobathy or FloodAdaptModelBuilder\\data\\watersheds

FloodAdaptModelBuilder
  |-- _internal
  |    |-- ...
  |
  |-- data
  |    |-- topobathy
  |    |    |--dem.tif
  |    |
  |    |-- watersheds
  |        |-- watersheds.gpkg 
  |
  | -- FloodAdaptModelBuilder.exe
