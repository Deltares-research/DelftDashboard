Adding dem or watersheds data: 
1. make sure the names are exactly: 'dem.tif' or 'Watersheds.gpkg'
2. put it in FloodAdaptModelBuilder\\data\\TopoBathy or FloodAdaptModelBuilder\\data\\WaterSheds

FloodAdaptModelBuilder
  |-- _internal
  |    |-- ...
  |
  |-- data
  |    |-- TopoBathy
  |    |    |--dem.tif
  |    |
  |    |-- WaterSheds
  |        |-- Watersheds.gpkg 
  |
  | -- FloodAdaptModelBuilder.exe
